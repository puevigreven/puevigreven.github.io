Hi, I'm Pralay.

I am a Platform Engineer working at a healthcare startup, ChironX, where I worked on the problems at the intersection of machine learning and healthcare.

My technical interests are in Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing and Software Engineering. I try to put my research and engineering efforts to projects with positive societal impact, including solving healthcare and environmental challenges.

Before joining ChironX, I did my undergraduate at IIT Patna, with my thesis work supervised by Dr Pushpak Bhattacharyya. As a part of my thesis, I worked closely with Dr Shweta Yadav on Patient Severity Detection task using Natural Language Processing and developed text annotation tools that are still been used in AI-NLP-ML Lab, IIT Patna.
